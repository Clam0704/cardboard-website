As far back as I can remember I always wanted to create interesting, memorable, and engaging film projects. I graduated Manchester Metropolitan University’s (MMU’s) Filmmaking course in 2022 with First Class Honours, specialising in video editing, directing, videography and vision mixing.


With professional experience in these fields, I enjoy working both to a client’s commission and on solo projects. At the moment I have been building my short-film portfolio, and have a body of work in digitally-composited video art under my ‘Cardboard’ alias. I strive to make meaningful artwork with filmmaking, whether it’s an intricate, captivating character study or an fiercely abstract atmosphere
