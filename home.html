VISUALS
As well as short films, I make abstract video art collages.
These are visual only, and are meant to be played while listening to music.

Some visuals are commissioned for events, or by artists.
Pictured are 7-hour visuals I made for Locked In at Gorilla, Manchester (April 8th, 2023).

Now taking commissions for music videos, lyric videos, video art.
Visit ABOUT page for my email, to discuss terms and fees.

Movement is random and the compositions are unstable,
Making the visuals appear to synchronise with virtually any track.
